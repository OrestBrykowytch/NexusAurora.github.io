About Us

We are a group of volunteers who all strive to reach the same goal: make life multiplanetary. Together, as a community of likeminded people, of all different backgrounds, work as a collective to ensure the amelioration of humankind's existance in this solar system and beyond.

Winners

In 2020, we participated and achieved first place in the Mars Society Design Competition. With a total of 175 team applications from over a dozen countries, each team submitted designs for human travel to Mars. You can read our report here!
